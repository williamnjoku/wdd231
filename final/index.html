Site Plan: Travel Guide to Lagos, Nigeria

Site Name

Travel Lagos Guide

This name captures the purpose of the site, helping people explore Lagos. It’s simple, relevant, and memorable.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an informative and visually engaging travel guide to Lagos, Nigeria. It will provide recommendations on places to visit, travel tips, and dynamic features like a weather forecast and interactive image gallery.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist attractions I can visit in Lagos?
  • Is it safe to travel to Lagos and how do I get around the city?
  • What should I eat and where can I find good, affordable accommodation?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #005b96 – Used for headers, CTA buttons, and navbar background.
  • Accent Color: #6495ED – Used for highlights, icons, section backgrounds.
  • Text Color: #222222 – Used for all paragraph and general content text.
  • Background Color: #FFFEEF – Used for body and card backgrounds.
  • Footer Color: #002147 – Used for the footer.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraph content for readability.
  • Roboto Bold (700): Used for headings, section titles, and callouts.
